An alchemist who studied with Vice-Arch Alchemist Needo Byzaar. She specialises in spider venom, and the effects it has on humanoids. She was undertaking her own personal research before she was kidnapped in the night. She has really messy golden blonde hair, and a scar across her left cheek. She had a black eye when the party first met them.
